## LLM Usage Report (Week 3)

# Overview

The team used LLM tools as supporting productivity tools during Week 3. All project decisions, implementation, testing, and reviews remained under human responsibility.

# How LLM Tools Were Used

* Assisting with the transcription and formatting of customer meeting recordings into readable English transcripts.
* Improving the wording, structure, and readability of project documentation and reports.
* Supporting the preparation of Sprint Review summaries, reflections, and retrospective documents.
* Providing explanations of technical concepts and implementation approaches when needed.

# Human Oversight

All AI-generated content was reviewed and edited by team members before being included in project deliverables. The team remained responsible for all requirements, development work, testing, customer communication, and final project artifacts.

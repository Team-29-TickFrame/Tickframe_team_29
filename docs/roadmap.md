# Tickframe Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the planned Sprint-by-Sprint delivery of Tickframe. It is updated as the Product Backlog evolves and provides a high-level view of planned outcomes and tracked work items.

## Sprint 1

Milestone: Sprint 1 (https://github.com/Team-29-TickFrame/Tickframe_team_29/milestone/1)

Start Date: 2026-06-16
Finish Date: 2026-06-22

### Sprint Goal

Deliver the first usable Tickframe analytics workflow with live market monitoring, quantitative metrics, correlation analysis, and breakout alerts.

### Expected Outcome

Users can monitor cryptocurrency market activity, analyze key quantitative indicators, compare correlations between trading pairs, and receive breakout alerts through a unified interface.

### Planned Items

- US-03: View live market data (https://github.com/Team-29-TickFrame/Tickframe_team_29/issues/25)
- US-05: View detected signals (https://github.com/Team-29-TickFrame/Tickframe_team_29/issues/27)
- US-06: Analyze quantitative metrics (https://github.com/Team-29-TickFrame/Tickframe_team_29/issues/28)
- US-09: Compare cross-pair correlations (https://github.com/Team-29-TickFrame/Tickframe_team_29/issues/37)
- US-13: Receive high and low breakout alerts (https://github.com/Team-29-TickFrame/Tickframe_team_29/issues/49)
- PB-07: Prepare MVP v1 release and deployment (https://github.com/Team-29-TickFrame/Tickframe_team_29/issues/43)
